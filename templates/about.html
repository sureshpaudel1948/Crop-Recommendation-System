Our Crop Recommendation System is a pivotal 7th-semester project undertaken by students of Bsc.CSIT from Tribhuvan University, focusing on addressing critical challenges faced by farmers in Nepal. Many farmers struggle with selecting the right crops due to a lack of comprehensive knowledge about how various climatic conditions and soil qualities affect crop growth. This knowledge gap often leads to suboptimal harvests, lower income, and discourages farming in a country with immense agricultural potential. Our system utilizes advanced Machine Learning algorithms to analyze essential environmental factors such as temperature, humidity, soil nutrients, and other parameters to offer personalized crop recommendations. By providing farmers with accurate and actionable insights tailored to their specific conditions, our system aims to enhance agricultural productivity and income. It empowers farmers with the tools they need to make well-informed decisions, ultimately improving their yields and economic stability, and fostering a more sustainable and prosperous agricultural sector in Nepal.
